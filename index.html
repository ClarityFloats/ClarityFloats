Will I drown?

There is atleast 800lbs of epsom salt which will make it nearly impossible to drown. You float with ease on your back.

How warm is the water?

Around 93.5F which is roughly the same temperature as your skin.

What if I freak out?

You can open the door at anytime and can turn on the light in the tank if you would like.

Why 90 minutes?

We have found that 90 minutes is the best amount of time to get deep into the experience. Anything less you may not get the full effects.
